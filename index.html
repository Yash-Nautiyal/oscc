About OSCC

Ordnance Services Computer Centre

Empowering India's Defense Infrastructure Through Advanced Information Technology

Organizational Overview

The Ordnance Services Computer Centre (OSCC) operates under the Ministry of Defence, Government of India, as the premier IT support organization for the Ordnance Services Directorate. Established to modernize and streamline defense logistics and procurement systems, OSCC serves as the technological backbone of India's ordnance services infrastructure.

Core Mission

To provide secure, reliable, and efficient information technology solutions that enhance the operational effectiveness of ordnance services across all branches of the Indian Armed Forces, ensuring seamless integration of technology with defense logistics and procurement processes.

Strategic Functions

  • IT Infrastructure Management
  • E-Procurement Systems
  • Cybersecurity Operations
  • Inter-Service Coordination
  • Technical Training Programs
  • Compliance & Standards

GeM is a one-stop portal to facilitate online procurement of common use Goods & Services required by various Government Departments / Organizations / PSUs. It provides the tools of e-bidding, reverse e-auction and demand aggregation to facilitate the government users achieve the best value for their money.

Our organization can use GeM to procure various goods and services including equipment, uniforms, vehicles, IT equipment, and other operational requirements. The platform ensures transparency, efficiency, and cost-effectiveness in procurement processes.

Key features include: Direct purchase for items up to ₹25,000, competitive bidding for higher value items, vendor registration and verification, quality assurance, payment integration, and comprehensive reporting for audit purposes.

NIC is the premier science & technology organization of the Government of India in informatics services and information and communication technology (ICT) applications. It provides network backbone and e-Governance support to Central Government, State Governments, Union Territory Administrations, Districts, and other Government bodies.

NIC provides our organization with secure network infrastructure, data center services, application development, cybersecurity solutions, and digital transformation support. This includes maintaining secure communication networks, managing databases, and developing specialized software for our operations.

NIC provides comprehensive IT services including: Secure network connectivity, data center hosting, application development and maintenance, cybersecurity solutions, email services, video conferencing, and technical support for various digital initiatives undertaken by our organization.

DPP is a comprehensive framework that governs the procurement of defence equipment and services for the Indian Armed Forces. It outlines the procedures, policies, and guidelines for acquisition of defence systems, ensuring transparency, accountability, and efficiency in defence procurement.

Our organization may follow DPP guidelines for certain specialized equipment and security systems, especially when procuring advanced security technology, surveillance systems, and specialized equipment that require stringent quality standards and security clearances.

The DPP process typically includes: Requirement identification, feasibility study, technical evaluation, vendor selection, contract negotiation, quality assurance, delivery and acceptance testing, and post-delivery support. Each stage has specific timelines and approval requirements to ensure proper oversight.
